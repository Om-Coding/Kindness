About Novalayer Home Services

Not just chores. A mission to help homes—and people—feel better.

Our Story

Novalayer Home Services started with a simple idea: home services should be easier, more reliable, and friendlier. What began as a few friends helping out their neighborhood grew into a trusted solution for families across the area. We’re here to lighten the load and help people love their space again.

Our Mission & Values

Every home deserves care. Our mission is to provide helpful, honest, and high-quality support—every single time.

Care

We treat every house like it’s our own—with patience, precision, and pride.

Honesty

No tricks. Just honest work, clear pricing, and communication that makes sense.

Teamwork

We’re more than coworkers—we’re collaborators, problem-solvers, and friends.

Our Journey

  • 2024 — Novalayer 3D Prints

    Novalayer was born from a small group of friends who had access to 3D printers and wanted to sell their prints.

  • 2024 — Beginning of Business

    After months of patience and work, we finally achieved our goal of selling 3D prints.

  • 2025 — The Problem

    While the 3D prints were successful, we noticed a problem: many people struggled to focus on the website because of common household chores.

  • 2025 — The Solution

    We realized this was a widespread issue, so after much effort, Novalayer Home Services was founded to help.
